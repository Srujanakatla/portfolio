Vegetable Biryani

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup basmati rice
  • 2 cups mixed vegetables (carrot, beans, peas, potatoes)
  • 1 large onion, sliced
  • 2 tomatoes, chopped
  • 1/2 cup yogurt
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • 1 teaspoon ginger-garlic paste
  • 1 teaspoon cumin seeds
  • 1 bay leaf
  • 2-3 cloves
  • 2-3 cardamom pods
  • 1 teaspoon garam masala
  • 1 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • 1 teaspoon red chili powder
  • Salt to taste
  • Fresh coriander and mint leaves for garnish

Instructions:

  1. Rinse the rice thoroughly and soak it in water for 30 minutes. Drain and set aside.
  2. Heat oil in a large pan. Add cumin seeds, bay leaf, cloves, and cardamom pods. Sauté for a minute.
  3. Add sliced onions and sauté until golden brown.
  4. Add ginger-garlic paste and sauté for another minute.
  5. Add chopped tomatoes and cook until they turn soft.
  6. Add mixed vegetables, turmeric powder, red chili powder, and salt. Mix well.
  7. Add yogurt and cook for a few minutes until the vegetables are tender.
  8. Layer the soaked rice over the vegetable mixture. Sprinkle garam masala on top.
  9. Add 2 cups of water, cover the pan, and cook on low heat until the rice is fully cooked.
  10. Garnish with fresh coriander and mint leaves before serving.
